Revolutionizing Technical Whiteboards

MagNEGA Board is a full-sized magnetic whiteboard with a mechanical automated sliding eraser designed for engineering instructors, technical professionals, and students.

Request Demo

Problem

Traditional whiteboards are inefficient for engineering and technical professionals. Markers dry out quickly, produce unclear writing, and manual erasing of long-form solutions is time-consuming. This results in wasted time, interruptions during lectures, and increased cost due to frequent marker replacement.

  • Markers last only around two weeks of regular use
  • Manual erasing interrupts teaching flow
  • Unclear writing reduces student understanding
  • Frequent marker replacement increases costs

Our Solution

The MagNEGA Board is an automated magnetic whiteboard system featuring a mechanical sliding eraser that clears the board effortlessly. It is designed for technical environments where long-form problem solving is required.
